About Me

Hi! I am MD and fourth year Ph.D. student at Medical University of Lodz, Poland.

I am doing my Ph.D. at Department of Biostatistics and Translational Medicine and I am broadly interested in using statistics, bioinformatics and mathematical modeling to uncover the connections between DNA damaging agents in cancer and adaptive immunity. I am also interested in statistical design of clinical trials.

My research so far focused on radiation oncology and biomarker research, but I am also soon starting my residence in Hematology.
